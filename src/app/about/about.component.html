Flower Power

Voted Best Garden Center by Indy’s A-List, Flower Power Garden has been selling plants since the early 1960s. Spend some time wandering through our garden centers where you’ll find beautiful annuals, perennials, trees and shrubs.
Since opening, Flower Power has been known as an eclectic hub of garden-inspired home decor and gifts. The Garden Cottage at our Broadripple location is one of Indianapolis' favorite treasures. The Flower Power family boasts a reputation for excellent customer service and a Master Gardener program.
